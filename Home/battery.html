BATTERY

Our go-kart being an electric kart, the battery is our fuel. Electric supply is needed to power up the kart electronics, motor controller and hence the motor. This is provided for, by our battery pack. We deal with rechargeable battery packs, the trendiest in their class.

Along with the battery we need a smart BMS (Battery Management System) which continuously monitors all the different parameters of the battery including its cell’s temperature, voltage, charge and SoC. The BMS also ensures safe operation while the kart is running by protecting the battery against accidental short circuit, over current and thermal cut-off. While charging the BMS protects the battery from overcharging. Designing our own sophisticated BMS is this team’s target. We use BMS to monitor the battery parameters so that the pit crew and the driver is aware about the status of the battery.

In an extreme case of thermal runaway event the BMS is smart enough to isolate the battery, by cutting off the supply, and warn us in advance so we can be prepared to deal with the fire.
